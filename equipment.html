What are the I, IV, and V Chords?

The I, IV, and V chords are the three most used chords in each major key.

They refer to the three main chords used in many blues songs with the I chord being the tonic or root chord acting as the home base in a song.

Let's look at Stones Throw Records

Now with a slight glance at this lineup, there are some familiar faces to most: i.e. MF DOOM, Flying Lotus, etc. But with a deeper look there are plenty more gems within and affiliated that you may come across .

We can start with the founder, peanut butter wolf.


Real hip hop really does come from strange places. Chris cut, soon to be peanut butter wolf, was from a tiny suburb in San Jose. PBW was a 19 year old kid who mostly kept to himself until he met up with then 16 year old Charles Hicks aka Charizma. AA few years later, after rapping at high school talent shows and developing a few tapes and shows while finding their own sound, they landed a deal with Hollywood Basics. Eventually the deal would fall through after about a year and an albums worth of recorded material, Charizma was tragically killed right after they left the label to reach new heights. After some time and digging throughout the city, PBW would end up forming Stones Throw, which would soon become the home of Madlib, MF DOOM, Fat Jon, Flying Lotus, and plenty more.

Why should you know all of this? Music is made from the darkest of places, and most of the time it deals with simply someone reaching out to someone else. That doesn't take away from all the trash surrounding it. All I can tell you is to keep looking in at the world of hip hop, there's so much you're able to learn about all genres of music.
